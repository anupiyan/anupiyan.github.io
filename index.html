About

Making ML systems legible, not just performant.

I'm an AI engineer with a PhD in AI from Murdoch University, where my research centred on computational pragmatics and natural language understanding — how meaning is inferred beyond literal text. That foundation now shapes how I think about explainability: not just what a model predicts, but why, and how to communicate that clearly to the people who rely on it.

Day to day, I lead AI/ML engineering at Qoria, and I conduct research with my team of research students as an Adjunct Senior Lecturer at Murdoch University. Alongside that, I'm building Darsha — an SDK for ML explainability and compliance, named for the Sinhala word for vision and visibility.
